Hypothesis

Visualizing and creating understanding of biosignals collected by physical tracking can enable users to make educated changes in their everyday life. In this sense, physical tracking is utilized to interpret one’s emotion and mood.

Objective

Create a mobile app which syncs to a wearable device to extract real-time data. Emotions and moods derived from biosignals will be used to create digestible visualizations and allow tracking of daily states & activities.

Project Goals

Clarify user’s patterns of moods and emotions over time using real-time data visualization Assist in tracking and understanding their states over time to make positive life changes Reach personal data when and where it’s needed
