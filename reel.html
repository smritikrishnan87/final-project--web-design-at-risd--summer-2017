Project Statement

The Reel is a mobile entertainment platform for sports fans to see premium highlights from hundreds of their favorite sports daily.

• The Reel connects sports fans with the content they want, when and where they want it.
• Fans receive a custom experience and access to top highlights, scores, and updates.

Solution Statement

The Reel gets to know you, and curates a custom experience based on what you follow, watch, search for, and more.

• The Reel is designed to support agile features and actions. Fans will experience a lightweight application that is heavy in desired content.

Project Goals

Immerse Instantly connect users to the latest premium highlights and reels based on preferences and smart suggestions Capture Create comfortable environment to seamlessly experience videos back-to-back, react, comment on, and share content Entice Craft sleek visual design and simple interactions to attract and delight users, and encourage ample use
