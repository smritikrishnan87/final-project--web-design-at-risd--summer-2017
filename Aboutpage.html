My Story

Originally from Rhode Island. An apparel major at Rhode Island School Of Design. I dabbled in various types of art and enjoyed every second of it. I find what interest me the most are the little things such as a beauty marks on a persons face or the way a medium just moves of the page.Whatever it is I try to capture it in my art.
